Pizza Recipe

Description

Pizza is a classic recipe, and it's fairly easy to make!

You will need:

  • Pizza dough
  • Garlic oil
  • Pizza toppings

Steps:

  1. Place dough in well-oiled bowl and cover with a damp cloth
  2. Set aside to rise until doubled, for about 1 hour
  3. Preheat grill for high heat an brush the grate with garlic oil
  4. Make pizzas
  5. Place one piece of dough on hot grill
  6. Brush garlic oil over crust
  7. Add toppings
  8. Cook until cheese melts
